Research Interests

Design of physical layer algorithms for wireless communication networks, in particular:

  • Efficient channel state acquisition, representation and feedback in MIMO and Massive MIMO systems
  • Data-driven approaches and machine learning applied to the physical layer of wireless communications
  • Design of non-coherent modulations and unsourced random access
  • Channel reciprocity modeling and calibration

My research involves tools from signal processing, information theory, random matrix theory, differential geometry, stochastic geometry, Bayesian and entropy-based inference, message-passing and graph signal processing techniques.
